Nigeria Egusi

What a nicely made Nigeria Egusi looks like.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • 1/4 cup of palm oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger paste
  • 1 can (400g) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of spinach or bitter leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1-2 cups of assorted meats (beef, goat, chicken)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat the palm oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add the minced garlic and ginger paste, and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add the ground egusi and stir well to combine.
  6. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  8. Add assorted meats and spinach or bitter leaf. Cook for an additional 10 minutes.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.
