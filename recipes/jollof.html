Nigeria Jollof

What a nicely made Nigeria Jollof looks like.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of ginger paste
  • 1 can (400g) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  4. Add the minced garlic and ginger paste, and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes and tomato paste, and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add thyme, curry powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  7. Add the rinsed rice and stir to coat with the tomato mixture.
  8. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth and bring to a boil.
  9. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until rice is cooked.
  10. Stir in mixed vegetables and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  11. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
